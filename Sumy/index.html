Get Started with Sumy

Sumy will give you a summary, keywords with definitions, and YouTube videos related to the text entered. If you want to try Sumy, then insert a Website URL, Upload a file, or Upload Text Manually.

About Sumy

Sumy was programmed and copyrighted by Austin Bouley in order to help himself and other visual learners retain information easier. Austin always had a hard time with reading comprehension; therefore, in order to assist him learn information quicker and easier, he created Sumy to summarize long passages, give him keywords and definitions to memorize, and YouTube video recommendations because he learned better from watching. Through his research, he learned that 65% of the population is visual learners; therefore, Austin Bouley hopes he can assist others in learning and retaining information to the best of their ability: visually.
